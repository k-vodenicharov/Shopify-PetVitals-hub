# PETVITALS Brand Guidelines

Brand personality:

* modern
* trustworthy
* emotionally warm
* premium
* minimal
* pet-lover focused

Brand tone:

* confident
* clear
* believable
* emotionally engaging
* concise

Avoid:

* exaggerated marketing claims
* hype language
* fake luxury wording
* spammy ecommerce phrases

Never use phrases like:

* "Premium Quality"
* "Best Product"
* "Amazing Solution"
* "Must Have"
* "Revolutionary"
* "High Quality"

Preferred copy style:

* benefit-driven
* emotionally specific
* realistic
* easy to scan
* customer-focused

The brand should feel like:
"A real modern pet wellness company."

Not:
"A generic AliExpress reseller."
